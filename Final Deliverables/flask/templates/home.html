What is Web Phishing?

Phishing is a type of social engineering attack often used to steal user data, including login credentials and credit card numbers. It occurs when an attacker, masquerading as trusted entity, dupes a victim into opening an email,instant message,or text message The recipient is tricked into clicking a malicious link, which leads to the installation of malware, the freezing of the system as part of a ransomware attack or the revealing of sensitive information.

Types of Phishing

There are many forms of attacks by which attackers can steal data.Some of them are

  • Whaling
  • Smishing
  • Vishing
  • Email phishing
  • Spear phishing
  • Angler phishing

Steps to prevent Web Phishing

To keep our data safe when browsing or when using the internet, the following points need to be remenbered

  • Be aware of suspicious sites
  • Verifying a site's authenticity
  • Checking online accounts regularly
  • Using firewalls
  • Never give personal info on the internet
  • Use a web phishing detector
